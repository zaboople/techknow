Notice how I can vertically center text & checkbox SO THAT THEY LINE UP CORRECTLY

The trick is to use: display:inline-block; vertical-align:middle

        <div style="margin:0.4em; padding:0px;">

            <div style="display:inline-block; vertical-align:middle">
                <input type="checkbox" class="checkbox" name="physicianNotProvided"
                    id="physicianNotProvided" ${physicianNotProvided ? 'checked':''}
                    style="margin:0px; padding:0px; background-color:blue;"/>
            </div>

            <div style="display:inline-block; vertical-align:middle">
                <label>Ordering clinician is not provided, or not available within the clinic</label>
            </div>

        </div>
